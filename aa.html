About Us

According to the cooperative principle, this organization has been established with the objective of improving the socio-economic condition of the members by providing loans at reasonable interest rate to the needy members through SIP based income generation program Collecting small amounts of savings among the members as savings and investing in the members themselves as a cooperative for mutual support, harmony and collective welfare as a community-based organization.
